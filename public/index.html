Built-in trading rules

Options entry

5M candle closes above 15M resistance + VWAP, volume is greater than 2x the last 10 candles, and MACD 8/17/9 crosses bullish with green histogram.

Stock swing entry

Good volume means price up 3–5% or more, volume 3x average, and close near the high. Best setups are breakout retest, volume base breakout, or trend + EMA dip.

Avoid

Distribution volume, rejected highs, extended 30–40% moves in two weeks, POC still above price, weak volume candles, or earnings within two weeks.
